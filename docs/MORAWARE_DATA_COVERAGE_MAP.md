## Standing architecture rules (recap)

- **Code** in GitHub; **business data** in Supabase; **sensitive logic** in `backend-core`; **secrets** in Vercel env vars.
- Do not permanently hardcode volatile business data (colors, tiers, pricing, SOPs, etc.).
- **Frontend route hiding is not security** — every API must enforce permissions server-side.

## 2. What is most useful in Supabase today

### `brain_jobs`

- **Identity & CRM-ish context:** `job_id`, `job_name`, `account_id`, `account_name`, `salesperson_name`, `creation_date`, `job_status`.
- **Scope:** `worksheet_sqft`, `form_count`, `field_count`.
- **Notes:** job-level `notes` when synced.

### `brain_fields`

- **Normalized label frequency** drives quoting and production semantics: the deep audit outputs `top_100_normalized_labels` and **category buckets** (material/color, slab, Sq.Ft., edge, sink, quote/price, etc.).
- **Per-job values** tie a job to customer/project detail that is not always on `brain_jobs` columns.

### `brain_forms`

- **Form/template distribution** — which Moraware templates dominate (see `top_template_names`, `distinct_jobs_per_template_top` in the deep audit).

### `brain_job_activities`

- **Activity type/status** and **start_date** ranges for flow widgets.
- **Text-derived buckets** in the audit (template, order stone, saw, Titan program, fabrication, install, CS, repair, etc.) — useful for Titan and floor visibility; not a substitute for business rules for “stuck” jobs.

### `brain_job_operational_summary`

- **Boolean signals** (`has_*`) and **`slab_numbers`**, **`operational_notes_text`**, **`raw_json`** when operational sync ran.
- **Gap signal:** `top_missing_or_empty_columns` in the audit shows which summary fields are often null/empty — candidates for richer Moraware inclusion or better normalization (plan first; no sync rewrites in this phase).

## 3. What is missing or uncertain

| Need | Gap |
| --- | --- |
| **“On Titan/saw today”** | No single Brain column; infer from `brain_job_activities` (and dates) plus shop calendar rules. |
| **Material color on the job row** | Often only in `brain_fields` labels/values; may need a **resolver** or denormalized column (additive, post-plan). |
| **Quote totals / won-lost** | May appear in fields/forms; not guaranteed standardized — Partner Quoting Platform will need **Supabase quote tables** long-term. |
| **Inventory / slab availability** | Not in Moraware alone — future **SlabSmith / inventory_bridge** tables. |
| **Held / stuck** | Requires **rules** (dwell time in status, phase mismatches), not raw export. |
